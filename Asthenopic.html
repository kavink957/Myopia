Asthenopic Symptoms

Did you know?

Asthenopic symptoms are related to eye strain or visual discomfort. They are caused due to activities such as reading, using a computer, or other near work.

How can Asthenopia affect me?

Prolonged near work:

Activities that demands intense focus for extend periods. Eg. Reading or Digital devices.

Prescription:

If uncorrected or wearing glasses or Contact lenses with an outdated or wrong prescription.

Poor lighting:

Insufficient or improper lighting environment.

With Asthenopia you will experience...

Eye Fatigue:

A feeling of tiredness or heaviness in the eyes.

Headaches:

Often centered around forehead or temples.

Blurred Vision:

Mostly temproary.

Eye Strain:

Discomfort or soreness in and around the eyes.

Dry eyes:

A sensation of dryness, grittiness, or burning in the eyes.

Double Vision:

Either constantly or intemittently.

Difficulty Focusing:

On shifting focus between near and far objects.

Asthenopia is common , but it can be avoided!!

Appropraite glass correction.

Take breaks during prolonged periods of close work.

Adjust lighting conditions

Maintain visual hygiene: 20-20-20 Rule i.e, Look at 20 feet far for 20 seconds every 20 minutes
